## 🧩 Core Concept: Vortex Fluid Particles

Entities represent **identifiable things with fixed aspects** - documents, datasets, web pages, ideas, or any resource that can be described. In the toroidal vortex model, entities are the **fluid particles** that circulate through the system, carrying information and maintaining provenance.

**Key characteristics in vortex dynamics:**
- ✅ **Identifiable** - has a URI for tracking through vortex
- ✅ **Describable** - can have properties and metadata  
- ✅ **Traceable** - maintains provenance through circulation
- ✅ **Immutable state** - represents specific vortex state at specific time
- ✅ **Fluid particle** - flows through activities and transformations
